Creating Lists in HTML

There are times you will find you need to organize your content into neat little lists. Well, HTML allows you to do this too! Two lists I will show off are: Ordered Lists, and Unordered Lists.

Ordered Lists

Ordered lists (ol) are simply lists that begin at the number one (1) and continue until you have entered all list items. For example:

  1. Milk
  2. Eggs
  3. Butter
You wrap your <li> <li> tags with the <ol> <ol> tags like so:

<ol>
  <li></li>
  <li></li>
  <li></li>
</ol>

Unordered Lists

Unordered lists (ul) are simply bulleted lists that can display your list of items in whatever order you specify. For example:

  • Milk
  • Eggs
  • Butter
You wrap your <li> <li> tags with the <ul> <ul> tags like so:

<ul>
  <li></li>
  <li></li>
  <li></li>
</ul>
