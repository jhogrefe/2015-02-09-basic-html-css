Scream Your Content and Organize It!

When you are ready to add some content, you can easily organize it using paragraphs and applying header styles to grab a reader's attention.

Paragraphs

A paragraph should not contain tables and other block elements.

The Paragraph tags are:

<p></p>

Headers

There are six levels of headers, beginning with h1 and growing smaller when using h6.

The Header tags are:

<h1></h1>

<h2></h2>

<h3></h3>

<h4></h4>

<h5></h5>
<h6></h6>
